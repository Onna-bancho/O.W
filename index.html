SUNG JIN WOO(성진우)


Sung Jin Woo(성진우)

is the protagonist of Solo Leveling. He was previousely
known as the World's Weakest Hunter and delved
down the path of becomming the the World's Strongest
Hunter he was Reawakened and became a player.


Jin Woo was reawakened as Player after surving the
Cartenon Temple and completing the secret quest
"Courage of the Weak" the mysteriouse system has al-
lowed Jin Woo to limitless level up and increases his
strength unlike all humans who are unable to increase
their strength unless reawakened.
